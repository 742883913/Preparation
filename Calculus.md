# Formulas and Qualities

## Formulas for Logarithms and 

$$\begin{cases}
  \log _a(xy)=\log _ax+\log _ay\\
  \log _a\frac{x}{y}=\log _ax-\log _ay\\
  \log _ax=\frac{\ln x}{\ln a}
\end{cases}$$

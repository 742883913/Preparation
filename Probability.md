### Formula

$$\begin{pmatrix}
  n\\
  k
\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}
  n\\
  n-k
\end{pmatrix}$$

$$n\begin{pmatrix}
  n-1\\
  k-1
\end{pmatrix}=k\begin{pmatrix}
  n\\
  k
\end{pmatrix}$$

$$\begin{pmatrix}
  m+n\\
  k
\end{pmatrix}=\sum _{j=0}^k\begin{pmatrix}
  m\\
  j
\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}
  n\\
  k-j
\end{pmatrix}$$
